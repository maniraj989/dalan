📋 Terms & Privacy

About Dalan

Dalan is a single-vendor Nepalese restaurant and food delivery app operated from Kathmandu Valley, Nepal. We specialize in authentic Thakali Thali sets, fresh Buff Momos, artisan chai, and traditional Nepali desserts.

Terms of Service

By using the Dalan app, you agree to provide accurate delivery information. Orders once placed cannot be cancelled after they enter the 'Preparing' stage. Dalan reserves the right to refuse service in case of fraudulent activity.

Payment Policy

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD) and eSewa digital payments. eSewa payments are processed securely through eSewa's official gateway. Dalan does not store any payment credentials.

Delivery Policy

Delivery is available within Kathmandu Valley. Standard delivery fee is Rs. 50. Orders above Rs. 500 qualify for free delivery. Estimated delivery time is 15–30 minutes depending on your location.

Privacy Policy

Your name, phone number, email, and GPS location are collected solely for the purpose of processing and delivering your order. We do not sell or share your personal data with any third parties. Data is stored securely on Supabase's encrypted infrastructure.

Contact Us

For support, complaints, or feedback, reach us at: dalan.kitchen@gmail.com or call +977-9801234567.
